Welcome to Sample-IT

SAMPLE IT is the fastest, easiest and safest method of sending samples all over the world with a single contact person.

  A little bit about us

SAMPLEITprovides comprehensive sample management services that cover the entire cycle from order to shipment through storage, handling, fractionation, packaging, labeling and documentation. Tecnisample has a long experience in the management of all types of samples including dangerous goods and / or controlled temperature and a deep knowledge of the regulations and peculiarities that order the sector, which allows us to stand out from the competition as the only interlocutor for everything related to sample management.

Maximize profit by outsourcing this service : you will get shorter shipping times, fewer incidents and waste, and benefit from reduced shipping costs thanks to the economies of scale and the agreements we have with all the major logistics operators.

   Advantage

   Your company will obtain significant cost savings by outsourcing sample management: free up precious space in your structure to dedicate to your business.

   You will not need to have specialized personnel in handling dangerous goods and / or international shipments.

   You will have the best transportation rates thanks to our volume agreements with carriers.

   You will save on the cost of packaging whatever the type.

   You will work with a single supplier with the logistical and administrative advantages that this entails.

    You will have fewer errors and incidents by ensuring that your shipments are properly packaged, labeled and documented.

   You will have less waste by keeping an exhaustive control of your inventories.
